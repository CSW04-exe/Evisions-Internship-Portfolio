Getting around

  • The top navigation bar moves between the six main sections: Home, About, Projects, Reflection, Resume, and Contact.
  • Inside Projects, the left sidebar switches between Department Weeks, Mock Product, Capstone, Bonus Courses, and Shadow Days. Breadcrumbs at the top of each page show exactly where you are.
  • Each department week has Previous / Next links at the bottom to move through all seven in order.
