Week 7 · Customer Success & Sales

Customer Success, Feedback Design, and Sales Technical Teams

01Summary

Closed out the rotation with Customer Success and Solutions Engineering — the team that advocates for the customer after the sale, and the team that proves a solution will actually work before it’s sold.

02What I Worked On

  • Feedback designDesigned a feedback survey using four principles — specific, anchored to a concrete feature, diagnostic, and focused on the work rather than the person — and mapped the full feedback lifecycle from plan to close-the-loop.
  • Sales demo practicePracticed a software demo built around a story instead of a feature tour, and built a customer persona (Amanda) to ground a sales conversation in a real value wedge: important, unique, and provable.

03Reflection

Products built without early customer feedback create more confusion after launch than products tested early with real users. And trust in a demo or a sales call gets built the same way it does anywhere else — by admitting what you don’t know and following up with a straight answer.
