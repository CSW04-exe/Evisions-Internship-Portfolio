Bonus Course

Business

Understanding how a company actually creates and protects value

01Summary

The business track took the abstract idea of “how a company works” and made it concrete, showing how every department at Evisions contributes to one shared outcome: creating value for a customer and then protecting that value over time.

Sessions moved from basic mechanics (how money flows between Sales, Product, Engineering, and Finance) to the people responsible for revenue, then hiring, then legal risk and contracts.

02Sessions & Topics Covered

  • Guest speaker seriesSat in on sessions from the CFO on financial modeling, the CRO on sales and revenue, and the CSO on the fundamentals of how a B2B software company is structured.
  • Product strategy funnelStudied how product ideas get filtered through four lenses: customer feedback, market signal, business impact, and feasibility, and why choosing what not to build is the hardest part.

03Reflection

Two ideas repeated all summer: no single team creates value alone, and leadership means making the best call with incomplete information and learning quickly. That reframed how I read every department after: not as a silo, but as one handoff in a longer chain.
