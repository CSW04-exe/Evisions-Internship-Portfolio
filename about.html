Career Goals

I consider myself well-rounded within my degree and familiar with a variety of technical areas, but some of my most valuable college experiences came from classes outside my major, alongside my senior capstone and my AI and Data Science coursework. Classes like calculus and technical writing and editing stand out as some of my most engaging experiences, where the work felt meaningful and I connected well with both the content and my peers.

My interests span software engineering, QA, web design, and AI, though I’m especially drawn to math, writing, and solving complex problems, as well as hands-on work. At this point in my journey, I see myself at a crossroads, still exploring where these interests fit together. I know that learning didn’t end with school, and I want to keep exploring, building, and growing into whatever direction feels right. This mindset is part of what drew me to this internship — I was hoping it could help clarify some of these career questions by giving me hands-on exposure to different departments and ways of thinking about the field.

Evisions

Evisions has spent over 25 years exclusively serving higher education, building software that eases the administrative load on colleges and universities so staff can focus on learning and discovery. The company currently supports over 900 institutions worldwide and is especially known for its highly rated customer support. Their core solutions include Argos, a flagship enterprise reporting tool, along with tools for document management and payment processing that integrate with the systems a school already uses.
