Week 4 · Marketing

Marketing, Sales, and Communications

01Summary

Rotated through Marketing, Sales, and Communications: the functions responsible for communicating a product’s value and turning that value into revenue.

02What I Worked On

  • Brand & personaBuilt a buyer persona and brand voice guide for the SEA product, then wrote a full content set around it: a case study, an industry blog post, product-launch and webinar emails, paid ad copy, and social posts.
  • Event & email planningPlanned a user-group conference (budget, format, and success metrics) and mapped out an email send calendar across time zones.

04Reflection

Revenue comes from creating value. A sale closes when the gap between where a customer is and what the product delivers feels real and worth paying for. Selling well looked a lot less like pitching and a lot more like asking better questions.
