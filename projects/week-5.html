Week 5 · Support & DevOps

Support, DevOps, and Cybersecurity

01Summary

Spent the week with Support, DevOps, and Security: the team that resolves customer issues and the team that protects the systems everything else runs on.

02What I Worked On

  • Ticket triagePracticed triaging support tickets in Salesforce with JIRA-linked defects, and worked through SLA design: priority tiers and escalation paths.
  • Security tabletopRan a ransomware tabletop exercise and reviewed AI-specific security threats (prompt injection, model poisoning, shadow AI) alongside compliance frameworks like SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, and FERPA.
  • Phishing exerciseWrote a phishing-email exercise from the attacker’s side to see what actually makes one convincing.

03Preview

Deliverables from this week, as submitted.

Support SLA & Escalation Plan

Priority tiers, escalation paths, and a pricing/licensing model for the West Coast support org.

Support Org Chart & KB Articles

Team structure and knowledge-base articles built to support the SLA plan.

Phishing Email Exercise

Written from the attacker’s side to study what actually makes a phishing email convincing.

04Reflection

AI expands what a support analyst can attempt, but it still produces confident wrong answers. Responsibility for accuracy stays with the human, not the tool. The ransomware exercise made clear that “pay or do not pay” is barely a financial question at all; it is a trust and legal one.

  • SLA Design
  • AI Security
  • Compliance (SOC 2 / HIPAA / FERPA)
  • Incident Response
