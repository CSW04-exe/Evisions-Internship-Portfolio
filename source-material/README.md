# Source Material

Raw internship deliverables, session notes, and planning documents used as the source for this
site's content. Nothing here is referenced by the live site or deployed with it — it's kept for
reference and to trace site content back to the original work.

## Structure

Alphabetically:

- **`About/`** — background/bio notes and the original resource deck behind `about.html`
- **`Contact/`** — notes behind `contact.html`
- **`Home/`** — notes behind `index.html`
- **`Internship_Key_Learnings.pdf`** — summary notes spanning the whole internship
- **`Projects/`**
  - **`Bonus/`** — one folder per Friday bonus course with a distinct handout: `Business/`, `Communications/`, `Philanthropy/` (Connections had no separate handout, so there's no folder for it)
  - **`Departments/`** — one folder per department week, `Week 1 - Product & UX/` through `Week 7 - Customer Success Team & Sales/`, each holding that week's notes and deliverables
- **`Reflection/`** — notes behind `reflection.html`
- **`Resume & CV/`** — source resume PDF
- **`Structure.pdf`** — original site structure/outline
